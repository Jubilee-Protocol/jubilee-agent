# Parachurch Ministry Growth Roadmap

## Phase 1: Governance & Accountability
- [ ] **Independent Board**: Recruit 3-5 independent directors (non-family, non-employees).
- [ ] **Audit/Review**: Contract a CPA for an independent financial review.
- [ ] **ECFA Self-Assessment**: Review the 7 Standards of Responsible Stewardship.

## Phase 2: Ecclesiastical Integration
- [ ] **Church Audit**: List the top 10 churches your members attend.
- [ ] **Pastor Consultations**: Use the drafted "Partnership Proposal" to seek feedback.
- [ ] **Resource Alignment**: Ensure your programs fill a gap rather than duplicating church services.

## Phase 3: Strategic Expansion
- [ ] **Three-Year Forecast**: Calculate the "cash gap" (operational costs vs. projected donations).
- [ ] **Ethical Fundraising Policy**: Formally ban commission-based fundraising.
- [ ] **Succession Plan**: Document core processes to mitigate "Founder Syndrome."

- **Counsel**: Proverbs 15:22
- **Stewardship**: Luke 14:28-30
- **Unity**: 1 Corinthians 12:12-27
- **Integrity**: 2 Corinthians 8:21
- **Testing**: 1 Thessalonians 5:21
